# 🐾 Barkway

**Smart, simple booking software built by groomers, for groomers.**

Welcome to the official GitHub organisation for **Barkway** - the pet grooming platform designed to make running your
business a walk in the park (well... a well-groomed one 🐶✂️).

---

## 🌟 Our Mission

At Barkway, we believe pet groomers deserve software that’s:

- **Simple** – Easy to use, intuitive, and stress-free.
- **Smart** – Automates the boring stuff so you can focus on the pets.
- **Supportive** – Built hand-in-paw with real groomers to solve real problems.

Our mission is to **help grooming businesses grow while giving owners more time to do what they love**.

---

## ✨ What We Do

Barkway is the all-in-one platform for any pet services businesses.

We take care of:

- 📅 **Scheduling** – Book appointments in seconds with our Smart Scheduler™
- 🐕 **Client & Pet Records** – Detailed profiles, notes, and photos
- 💳 **Payments & Invoicing** – Deposits, balances, and polished PDFs
- 🔔 **Reminders & Recalls** – Automated texts and emails to keep tails wagging
- 📊 **Insights** – Reports that help you understand and grow your business
